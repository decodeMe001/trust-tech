About our Automatic Change Over switch.

Replacing manual change-over systems, Automatic Change-Over Switch System (ACS) helps consumers to define their new electricity auto switch system. ACS is a device that switches off a generator and changes to mains current automatically without the need for you to doit manually.

  • Automatic change over from generator to mains current
  • Automatically switches off your generator
  • Protects home appliances from power surge
  • Uninterrupted power switching system
